[SciPiPol](https://scipipol.com) was a platform for connecting scientists and policymakers to collaborate on pending legislation, built to strengthen evidence-based policy making by giving domain experts a structured way to engage with bills as they moved through the legislative process. Live bill data was pulled from LegiScan and surfaced alongside collaboration tools matching scientific expertise to the specific bills where it was most relevant.

## Technical Foundation
A containerized Django and PostgreSQL stack, with LegiScan as the upstream source for legislative data.
